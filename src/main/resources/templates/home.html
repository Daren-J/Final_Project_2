Biography:

My name is Daren James. I was born in the Bronx, New York. I was a happy kid, until one day in middle school my dad got arrested for killing my mom. It changed my life forever, and from that day forward I wanted to be a forensic investigator so I could put people like my father in jail. One day, the particle accelerator in my city exploded. At the same time, I was working in my lab. I got hit by a bolt of lighting, while some chemicals fell on top of me. I was knocked into a coma, and didn't wake up for days. When I did, I found that something about me changed. I was fast, really fast and could move at superspeed. From that day forward, I became a hero, a symbol of justice, the fastest man alive. I am the flash.
